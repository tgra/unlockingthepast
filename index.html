About

Step into the past and explore history like never before with our digitised collection of historical documents.

Unlocking The Past brings the stories of the past directly to your fingertips. Whether you’re tracing your family tree, delving into key historical events, or simply curious about the world as it once was, our intuitive tools make it easy to search, browse, and uncover fascinating details.

IIIF
IIIF is a set of open standards for delivering high-quality, attributed digital objects online at scale. It’s also an international community developing and implementing the IIIF APIs. IIIF is backed by a consortium of leading cultural institutions.
Linked Data
Linked data is a method of structuring and connecting data across different sources on the web, making it easily discoverable and interoperable.
Globally Persistent Identifiers
Unlocking The Past uses globally persistent identifiers for people, places and events, providing stable, unique references that ensure consistent and reliable connections across datasets over time.
