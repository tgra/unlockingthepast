SATURDAY, 27 DECEMBER, 1941

NOTIFICATION OF A STATE OF WAR WITH BULGARIA.

His Majesty's Government in the United Kingdom have been informed by the Government of the United States, who were in charge of British interests in Bulgaria, that a declaration of war by that country on Great Britain

was announced in the Bulgarian Parliament on the 13th December. His Majesty's Government have so far received no official communication to this effect from the Bulgarian Government, but in view of the above information it is hereby notified that a state of war exists between the two countries as from the 13th December.
